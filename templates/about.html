Accountability: About This Data Set

This data set summarizes the the energy and water use for the 144 Allegheny County Operated Buildings from 2010-2014. The metrics Metrics include: kBtu (thousand British thermal units) site and source EUI (energy use intensity), annual electricity, natural gas and steam consumption and cost, and water and sewer use and cost. The data also includes weather-normalized which allows for building comparison over time, despite any unusual weather events that may have occured.

Where did this data come from?

This data set come from the Western Pennsylvania Regional Data Center (WPRDC) website. The original data in multiple formats can be found at https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/allegheny-county-energy-and-water-use.
